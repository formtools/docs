Permissions

{% include screenshot.html item="i292.gif" %}

An important part of this module is the permissions: who can see what visualization. We modeled the permissions after the Export Manager, which lets you map permissions to the form View and/or to particular client accounts.

The other permission-related setting is found on the Main Settings page. There, the "Allow clients to refresh cache" setting lets you control whether or not client accounts may update the cache for visualizations that appear in their Quicklinks Dialog.
