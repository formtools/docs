Template Sets

Template Sets control the look and feel of your published forms. They let you customize everything in your published forms - HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Like with Form Tools itself, the Form Builder walks that fine line between ease of use, and flexibility. With the Form Builder, you're seldom in the position where you can't make the form look how you want: it's just a question of how willing you are to spend time doing the customization.

Default template sets

The Form Builder comes with a number of Template Sets already set up for you to use. They don't need any additional customization in order to use and are immediately available when you published your first form. To best illustrate how these Template Sets look, the screenshots on this page are all of exactly the same form: they are simply rendered with different sets (1 click in the Form Builder!).
